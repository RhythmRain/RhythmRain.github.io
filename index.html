Rhythm Rain is a music rhythm game designed to procedurally generate a rhythm based level to the beats of your music.

  • Learn more

The Music

Experience the music of local Columbia College of Chicago independant artist with several tracks from up and coming artist.

Gameplay

Tap or swipe the falling raindrops to accurately hit the beats of the music.

iTunes Library

Experience the game using your own iTunes library of music. Generating a level based on your own music.

Who We Are

Rhythm Rain is brought to life by Neil Inglese and Natasha Gawrit. Based on Natasha's passion for music and love for game design. We wanted a way to bring not only recognition to Columbia College of Chicago's up and coming musicians, but to add a fun and engaging gameplay element to the mix. natasha brought the artwork and gameplay to the next level, while Neil was able to bring the game to life and create Rhythm Rain based on Natasha's designs.

Currently a student at Columbia College of Chicago, pursuing a degree in Computer Programming.
